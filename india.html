Our Mission

Our mission is to promote and bring awareness of the schooling system in India. Education is a vital part of a child's life and success yet millions of kids in India receive little to no school, thus cuasing the illteracy rate of India to rise. The Indian Express complied data amongest 2 million children,conlcuding that over 70% of Delhi government school students cannot read or do simple calculations. Therefore, we want to bring this issue to light to allow children in India to reach their fullest potential and get the education they deserve.

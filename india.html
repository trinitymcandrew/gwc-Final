Our Mission

Our mission is to promote and bring awareness of the schooling system in India. Education is a vital part of a child's life yet millions of kids in India receive little to no school. In addtion, instead of going to school, many girls have to stay at home and do housework. We want to show that girls should attend school and get the opportunity to learn. The Indian Express compiled data amongst 2 million children,concluding that over 70% of Delhi government school students cannot read or do simple calculations. Therefore, we want to bring this issue to light to allow children and girls in India to reach their fullest potential and get the education they deserve.

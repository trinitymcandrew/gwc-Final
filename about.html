Jenna and I are first cousins. I am 14 years old, and I live in San Jose, California while Jenna,16, lives in Scranton, Pennslyvania. Despite living on opposite sides of the country, every year we get together on a family vacation. Both of us stronglgly believe that education as an important part of life and that all kids should get an chance to recieve quality education. Our grandmother grew up in a small village called Sarban, India. We saw how our grandmother did not have the privilage of receiving quality education. After hearing this, we wanted to help children, especially girls, receive equal education. We hope to rasie awareness for child education with our vision.
